Activating the Account for Software Downloads

Software downloads of Liquibase Enterprise and DMC are maintained on download.liquibase.com.

  • Customers will get an email from notifications@thruinc.net, subject User Activation Notification (download.liquibase.com)
  • If you did not receive a registration email for download.liquibase.com and need to download Liquibase Enterprise or DMC please contact our Technical Support team to request access.

Support emails you an invitation to set up and activate the account.

  1. Click thelink in the email.
  2. Enter and confirm a password, then click SUBMIT.

    Please note the password guidelines on this page.



  3. When the password meets the guidelines, your account is activated. Click the here link to log in and access the site.

Accessing the Site

  1. In a browser, go tohttps://download.liquibase.com. Log in.

  2. Open FOLDERS, then COMMON.

  3. There are folders for Datical software. The key folders are these:
    • Datical_DB_Software - downloads for the Liquibase Enterprise and client.
    • Datical_Documentation - [%=General.LBEnterprise%] Documentation Home
    • Datical_DMC_Software - downloads for DMC

  4. If you are ready to download software, go to Downloading the Liquibase Enterprise Installer.
    • Otherwise, look around. Log out when you are done.
