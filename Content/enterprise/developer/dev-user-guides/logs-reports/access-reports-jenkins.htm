Accessing Reports in Jenkins

When you implement in a Jenkins ARA solution, the results and reports for operations are shown in Jenkins pages.

When you click a link for a report, the report is looses it's formatting and images. This is caused by administrative settings in Jenkins.

Solution

Download the report to a file, then open it in a browser.

  1. Right-click the link, then choose Save As.
  2. Choose the location for the file, then click Save.
  3. Open the file in a separate browser tab or page.
